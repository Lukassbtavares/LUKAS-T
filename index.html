02 — Selected Work

Projects

01
Mechanical Battery
Designed and developed a fully mechanical energy storage system converting gravitational potential energy from a 2 kg mass into stored torsional spring energy, subsequently releasing it to lift a 1 kg mass. Covered concept design, engineering calculations, CAD, manufacturing, and proof-of-principle testing.
SolidWorks Torsional Spring Gear Ratio Optimization FEA Manufacturing
↗
02
Robotic Arm Gripper — Cobot CR10
As team leader, I led the full development of a custom gripper system for the CR10 collaborative robot. I designed and 3D printed all custom gripper fingers and fixtures for precise, stable handling of cylindrical parts. Programmed the robot in DobotStudio using Lua scripting, controlling sorting and placement logic based on colour detection — delivering a fully functional, zero-error assembly line.
Team Lead DobotStudio (Lua) Dobot CR10 3D Printing Fixture Design SolidWorks
↗
03
Windmill-Powered Water Pump
Designed and built a windmill-powered water pump system capable of pumping up to three metres. Developed mechanical drawings, performed structural calculations on the main shaft, and validated blade design with CFD analysis. Manufacturing involved welding and turning.
SolidWorks CFD Analysis Stress Analysis Welding Turning
↗
04
Lightweight Bike & Dummy System ONGOING
Designing a lightweight bike and dummy system using minimal material while maintaining structural stability. Focused on material selection, structural optimization, and ensuring the design can withstand realistic loading conditions.
Structural Analysis Material Optimization CAD
↗
05
AR Environment — Aryzon.world Cell
Created an augmented reality environment of a U-shaped manufacturing cell using Meta Quest 3. Every component was modelled in CAD and integrated into the AR workspace, providing an interactive digital twin of the physical production setup.
Meta Quest 3 ARYZON.world Digital Twin CAD
